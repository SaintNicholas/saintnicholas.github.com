The USACO is a site that has programming challenges that users can submit solutions to.  The site judges the output of the program to determine if the solution was correct.  The challenges progressively get harder as more of them are solved.

More information can be found at the following links: \\
http://train.usaco.org/usacogate \\
http://web.me.com/dpiele/usaco/USACO.html
